Welcome to Shuai Yuan's Website

My Research Background

Throughout my studies, I’ve engaged in a variety of research projects, from molecular cloning and tissue culture to advanced data analysis and predictive modeling. My work emphasizes understanding disease mechanisms, studying epidemiological patterns, and building data-driven solutions for public health. I have hands-on experience in microbiology, virology, biostatistics, and healthcare management, and I’m proficient in R, Python, SAS, and Stata, which I regularly use for statistical analysis and computational modeling.
